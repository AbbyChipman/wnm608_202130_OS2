Being in college often means being short on time and money. Here are three recipes that are easy on the wallet as well as the schedule!

Breakfast - Cereal

What you'll need:

  • Cereal (I like the ones that turn the milk chocolatey
  • Milk
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

Directions:

  1. Pour cereal into bowl
  2. Pour milk onto cereal
  3. Use spoon to eat
  4. Enjoy the chocolate milk afterward!

Lunch - Tuna Sandwich

What you'll need:

  • 1 can or pouch of tuna (any style)
  • 1 TBSP Mayonnaise (enough for creamy texture)
  • Onion Powder to taste
  • Chopped Pickles (optional)
  • 2 slices of bread

Directions:

  1. Empty contents of tuna can/pouch into bowl
  2. Stir in mayonnaise
  3. stir in onion powder
  4. Add pickles if desired
  5. Spread mixture on bread
  6. Enjoy

Dinner - Chicken Noodles

What you'll need:

  • 1 package Lipton Pasta Sides - Chicken Flavor
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 TBSP butter or margarine (optional)

Directions:

  1. Bring water and butter/margarine to a boil
  2. Add contents of noodle package
  3. Cook on medium-high heat 5 - 7 minutes or until tender
  4. Let stand (no heat) for two minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy
